]]> Film Photography Podcast – Episode 331 – October 15, 2024 /

Mat Marrash hosts this exciting episode, airing on the 15th anniversary of the FPP and recorded at the annual Photography Pro Show, covering topics such as “why now is the most exciting time to shoot film,” as well as interviews with Tim Ryugo from Kodak, Matt Parry and Hilary Freed from Harman Photo / Ilford Film and Meredith Reinker, Cory Reinker and Josh Steele from Roberts / Used Photo Pro. Closing out the show Mat catches up with FPP’s Paige Davis and founder, Michael Raso.

The 2024 PRO* Convention and Trade Show* in Indianapolis, Indiana was a vibrant testimony to the endurance and growth of film photography and an incredible opportunity to meet face-to-face with the vendors whose passion for film is essential for a thriving film community.

*The Photographic Research Organization (PRO) is a cooperative buying group with hundreds of members across the US, Canada and the UK. The PRO convention brings together members and retailers to facilitate a dialogue on the state of the photo industry, cultivate community, and provide education and opportunity for growth.
